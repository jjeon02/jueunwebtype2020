Gulja

Gulja is a unit that does not exist in English. To make a word or sentence, gul-ja is necessary.

consonant + vowel

The most basic structure. This is the minimum structure that is required in order to make a word.
consonant + vowel + consonant

consonant + vowel + consonants

Consonant sound different depend on their location, and sometimes they're not pronounced. The maximum number of con-boxsonants that can be at the end of gul-ja is two.
double consonant + vowel

Double consonant can come at the on the front and back of pronounciation. However, one set of two consonants that are different alphabet can not come at the front.
double consonant + vowel + consonant

double consonant + vowel + consonants

Similar rule applies.
consonant + double vowel

Similar rule applies for gulja with double vowel.
consonant + double vowel + consonant

consonant + double vowel + consonants

Similar rule applies for gulja with double vowel.
double consonant + double vowel

This type of combination is also avaliable.
double consonant + double vowel + consonant

double consonant + double vowel + consonants

The last gulja is the most complex structure of gulja. No more vowels or consonats are not avaliable.

Word

Korean words are made up of gulja.

One Gulja

One Gulja is the minimun number element that makes word.
Multiple Gulja

double consonant + double vowel + consonants

Gulja can be combined and create either similar or different meaning. The meaning of word can depend on the Chinese Character the gulja is representing. Words with same spelling can have different meaning if they are different Chinese character.
Gulja with grammatic purpose

In Korean language, there are many different type of gulja that is purely for grammatic purpose. For example, every proper sentence need to end with a specific grammatic gulja that is for ending a sentence. However, some grammatic rule does not strictly applies for everyday conversation.
Multiple Gulja

There are no specific limit of gulja on one word but most of the time it is common for one word to have less than 10-12 gulja.
